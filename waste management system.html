Overview

The Waste Management System at LPU is dedicated to managing and reducing waste across campus. Our goal is to create a cleaner, greener, and sustainable campus by minimizing waste production and promoting recycling and proper disposal of waste.

Why Waste Management is Important

Proper waste management helps protect the environment, improves public health, and conserves natural resources. At LPU, we focus on reducing landfill waste, recycling materials, and educating students on environmental responsibility.

Our Waste Management Process

  • Collection: Separate bins are placed across campus for different types of waste (recyclables, organic waste, and non-recyclables).
  • Sorting and Recycling: Collected waste is sorted at our facility, and recyclables are processed accordingly.
  • Disposal: Non-recyclable waste is disposed of in an eco-friendly way to minimize environmental impact.

Donate Your Reusable Waste

Do you have items that can be reused instead of being thrown away? Donate them to support LPU's sustainability efforts!
